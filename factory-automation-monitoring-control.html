Factory Applications



Machinery Automation, Monitoring and Control

Unlock comprehensive machine monitoring and control capabilities with Neptune. From tracking machine tampering and breakdown alerts to managing service requests, Neptune empowers you to stay on top of your operations effortlessly. With two relay outputs, our controllers enable precise control over machine functionalities, allowing you to toggle operations and manage various aspects with ease. Stay connected and in control with WhatsApp integration, enabling seamless communication and control directly from your smartphone. And with FineTrack, gain centralized oversight of hundreds of devices simultaneously, ensuring you remain informed and in control of every aspect of your factory operations. With Neptune, streamlined monitoring and control are at your fingertips, revolutionizing the way you manage your industrial processes.

Tamper and Security

Our controller serves as a cornerstone in fortifying the security of factories and machinery, offering versatile functionality through its array of 6 digital inputs. These inputs are ingeniously employed, whether connected to panic buttons for swift response or wired to motion detectors that trigger relays, activating sirens and floodlights to deter intruders. With Neptune, these devices seamlessly interact, forming an interconnected ecosystem of control and automation unprecedented in its versatility. With a single device and software interface, you gain complete control over every facet of your factory, ensuring its safety even when you're away. Experience unparalleled peace of mind knowing that your security measures are seamlessly integrated and effortlessly managed with Neptune at the helm.

Custom Made Solutions

At Fine, we prioritize collaboration with our customers and innovators to develop tailored solutions that we offer to our clients, allowing them to rebrand and sell our devices under their own name. One such solution is our machine run time system, designed to optimize operational efficiency. This system triggers alerts when a machine has been running for a specified period, signaling the need for maintenance or refilling. Additionally, we provide buttons for reporting machine breakdowns and service requests, facilitating seamless communication with our FineTrack control room and users via WhatsApp. Through our collaborative approach and innovative solutions, we empower our clients to enhance their operations and deliver superior service to their customers.
