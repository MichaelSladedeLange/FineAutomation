The
NEPTUNE
6 inputs and 2 outputs GSM controller

This unit is powered a by 12V supply or battery and has six 12V inputs and two 2A relay outputs. It can store up to 400 numbers for drop-call activation of one reply output. SMS up to 7 numbers and GPRS number for control stations. Outputs have daily timers for scheduled activation.

Features

  • Powered by 12V power supply or gel battery.
  • Can store up to 400 individual drop-call numbers for output activation.
  • Can SMS up to 7 numbers with a GPRS number for control rooms.
  • Two 2A relays can be programmed to switch on/off at certain times of the day.
  • Six 12V inputs for water level sensor, temperature sensor, counter and more.
  • Programmable over the air with SMSes.

Applications

  • Pump and water/dam level control, temperature control, machinery on/off and more.
  • Can be used as a wired alarm system with 6 passives, door guards or panic button with siren activation.
