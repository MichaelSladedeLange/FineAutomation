    What Our Products Do

    Our devices use SMSes and phone calls to control outputs and read inputs which are then sent back to you. Therefore it can be used to remotely turn on/off generators, motors, machinery, pumps and more. Our systems also include security systems that are remotely activited/disamred and send messages to multiple numbers.

    Our Products

    GSM Controllers

    The Mercury

    Our reliable GSM battery monitor can handle input voltages from 1 volt to 70 volts. This unit has two inputs and one output. The output relay can handle 10 amps of current at 250 volts. It supports drop-call operations and therefore can be used for various different applications including gate access control and water pump control. The unit can store up to 400 numbers and be programmed over the air with SMSes.

    The Neptune 6 in 2 out

    The Neptune can read in 6 inputs and has 2 output relays that can handle 2 amps of current. It supports drop-calls and can be used for water pump control, gate access control and has built-in timers to control the outputs at various times.

    Security Systems

    The Venus Wireless Alarm System

    Our affordable wireless alarm system is easy to use and robust. It has 6 zones, each zone can handle two detectors, either a wireless passive or wireless doorguard. The detector batteries last from 3 to 5 years and will send a low battery message when a change is needed. The unit itself can last for up to 10 hours on its 12 volt battery. This system can be programmed over the air and is incredibly easy to install.
