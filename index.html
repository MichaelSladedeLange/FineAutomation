GSM Controllers

Our GSM controllers can be used to read inputs, turn on/off outputs and send or receive SMS commands to operate both itself and other units. These units are powered by either a 12V power supply or battery. Messages to and from the unit are done with either a prepaid or contract SIM card.
These controllers are used for pump and water/dam level control, temperature control, gate access control, battery level monitoring and more.

Alarm Systems

Our wired and wireless security alarm systems are controlled with either a remote or by sending SMSes to the unit. These system operate on a 12V power supply and 10 hour battery. Either a prepaid or contract SIM card can be used.
Our wireless systems are used for easy installation, temporary sites, renting and residential areas. Our wired alarm is used for wired applications and for medium to large sites.

Add-Ons

We provide many other supportive products such as batteries, housings, sirens, door guards sensors, temperature controllers, water level sensors and more. We also provide application specific solutions for pump and water/dam level control, temperature control, battery monitoring and gate access control.

How our products work

Our products use either prepiad or contract SIM cards to send and receive messages and calls. These messages are then used to control features on the unit, such as turning on/off or pulsing the output replays, reading inputs and sending values back, sending itself commands and sending other units commands. Therefore these systems can be used to create closed-loop feedback systems such as turning on/off a water pump with one unit while another is used to sense the water level.
