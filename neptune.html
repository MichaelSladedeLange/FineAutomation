    The Neptune ...

    Is a GSM unit that uses a SIM card to send and receive SMS messages or calls. These control the 6 outputs on the device that can then be used to switch on or off lights, motors, pumps or machinery. This unit also has two inputs that can send messages and toggle the outputs of the unit.

    Features

    • 6 digital inputs and 2 relay outputs
    • 4 of the 6 inputs can be configured as counters which can be requested by SMS
    • Loging of counters and email reports to up to 4 emails
    • Can SMS up to 7 numbers
    • Can send messages when inputs go high, low or both.
    • Inputs can be configured normally high or low
    • Inputs can have a debounce time from 1 second to 9999 seconds
    • Inputs can have a retrigger delay from 1 second to 9999 seconds
    • Inputs can send only to specific numbers if required
    • Outputs can be programmed to switch on/off or pulsed at various times of the day
    • Output pulse time can be configured
    • 1 amp relays on the outputs
    • All functions can be controlled with SMS or timers
    • 10 to 16 VDC operating voltage
    • Automatic low battery SMS. Voltages for low battery can be set
    • Fully programmable over the air via SMS or from a computer via EEPROM programmer
