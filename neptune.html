Sensors and Controls



W1209 Temperature Sensor

Stay in control of your environment with the seamless connection of Neptune and our dedicated temperature sensor unit. Get instant alerts via your phone the moment temperatures stray beyond your desired levels, ensuring peace of mind and timely action.

  • Input Power: 12 volts
  • Relay: Isolated 12v 10amp ouput
  • Temperature Range: -50 to 150 (degree Celsius)
  • Probe: PT100 temperature probe
  • Probe Length: 50cm

Setup is as easy as powering the sensor with 12V and bridging across to the relay input "K1". Then connect "K0" to any input on the Neptune.

Non-Invasive/Contact Water Level Sensor

Say goodbye to manually checking your tank and hello to effortless water level monitoring. This innovative, non-contact sensor simply sticks to the outside of your JoJo or Roto tank, accurately measuring the water level without ever needing to enter it.

  • Input Power: 5-24 volts
  • Output: Input (5-24 volts)
  • Response Time: 500ms
  • Operating temperature: 0-80 (degrees Celsius)
  • Cable Length: 50cm

To setup this sensor, power it with 12V, and connect the signal line to any of Neptune's inputs. It's that easy.

Water Flow Counter

Our flow counters seamlessly measure every liter passing through, with over-the-air calibration to ensure accuracy even in complex systems. Receive automatic notifications when water usage reaches specific thresholds and request the current flow count anytime, directly from the unit.

  • Input Power: 5-24 volts
  • Output: Input (5-24 volts)
  • Flow range: 0-60L/minute
  • Operating temperature: < 80 (degrees Celsius)
  • Operating water temperature: < 120 (degrees Celsius)
  • Water Pressure: < 1.75Mpa

Power the unit with 12V, then connect signal line to input 5 on the Neptune-W (water-count series).
