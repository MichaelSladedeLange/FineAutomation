Neptune 6 Input 2 Output GSM Controller

Our Neptune unit has 6 x 12V inputs that can be used for anything, from measuring temperature and water levels to sensing alarm signals and sending SMSes. It has 2 x 12V 2A relay outputs that can switch on chargers, motors or even larger relays for larger machinery use. It can store up to 400 drop-call numbers for relay one activation and can SMS up to 7 numbers including a GPRS number for control rooms. It is powered by 12V and/or any standard 12V battery. All messages are programmable.

Applications

The Neptune is extensively used on farms for pump and water/dam level control using inputs to measure water level and outputs to switch pump.

The 12V inputs are also used for temperature sensing, alarm or motion sensors and much more.
