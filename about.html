Fine Automation

Fine Automation was started late into the COVID pandemic by a father and son team. Around 2022 we bought and took control of part of an electronic business. From there we have started to build the business and focus on what our customers need. We are continuing to grow and expand our product offering, constantly searching for new and innovative products to offer our customers. We strive to stay up-to-date with the latest technologies and trends.
We strive to provide the best customer service we can by ensuring we communicate quickly and effectively, are always available during work hours for support and questions, have tons of online resources such as manuals, videos and summary cards. At the end of the day, we are where are because our customers choose to do business with us. It is therefore our focus to ensure those customers get everything they need.
We have set ourselves a set of values to ensure we uphold our customer service and growth into the future.

Our Values

Integrity

Accountability

Respect

Teamwork

Continuous improvement

Customer focus

These values ensure we focus our efforts on the three most important parts of our business. These are the customer and the customer experience, our products and ensuring we constantly update and grow with the times, and our own people, providing them with everything they need to grow as individuals and provide a great working environment to produce great work.
