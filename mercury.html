Mercury Battery Monitor, Gate Access Controller and Switch

Our Mercury unit has 2 inputs that measure voltages anywhere from 0-70VDC. It also has a single 12V 10A relay output making it perfect for applications of battery monitoring, sensor measurements, gate access control, pump control and much more. It can store up to 400 drop-call numbers for relay activation and SMS up to 7 numbers and a GPRS number for control rooms. Powered by 12V, it can run off any standard 12V battery. All messages are programmable.

Features

Powered by a 12V power supply and/or battery. Can store up to 400 individual drop-call numbers for gate access control. SMS up to 7 numbers with a GPRS number for control rooms.
Single 10A 12V relay can be programmed to switch at certain times of day. Both inputs can measure voltage levels from 0V up to 70V for battery monitoring. Programmable over the air with SMSes.

Applications

Gate access control with output used to pulse gate and inputs used for sensing if gate has opened or closed.

Monitor battery voltage by using the two inputs to read in voltages and the output to turn on/off a charger for one or both of the batteries.
