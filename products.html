Mercury Battery Monitor / Gate Access Controller

Used for battery monitoring or gate access control. Can store up to 400 drop-call numbers and can SMS up to 7 numbers. The 2 inputs can measure and send SMSes for any voltage levels from OV-70V. Comes with a 10A relay output. The unit is powered by either a 12V power supply or battery.

Venus Wireless Alarm System

Has 6 zones, each can have 2 passives allowing for 12 wireless passives or door guards to be connected. Controlled with remote or by sending SMSes. Powered by a 12V power supply with 10 hour battery reserve. Connections for buzzer, siren and radio transmitter. Can SMS up to 6 numbers and GPRS number.

Neptune 6 Input 2 Output Controller

Used for pump and water/dam level control, temperature control and other monitoring systems. Can SMS up to 7 numbers. The 6 inputs can measure from 0V to 12V. The unit is powered by either a 12V power supply or battery.
