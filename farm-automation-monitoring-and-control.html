Farm Applications



Management of Irrigation Systems

Our 4G controller stands as a cornerstone in farm automation, having found extensive use across diverse agricultural operations. Among its myriad applications, the Neptune controller shines brightest in optimizing irrigation systems, particularly those employing sprinklers and pivots. Boasting 6 digital inputs, our devices excel at monitoring pivotal aspects such as tampering and breakages in pivots, as well as managing their activation and deactivation. Moreover, our controllers are adept at precisely measuring irrigation volumes, ensuring optimal water usage. With the integration of our innovative WhatsApp feature, you gain unparalleled convenience in monitoring and controlling these systems directly from your smartphone. Alternatively, our dedicated FineTrack control room offers a robust solution for overseeing your operations, whether you're managing a single unit or an extensive network. With FineTrack, scalability is limitless, empowering you to streamline your farm's efficiency with ease.

Dam Level and Pump Control

In addition to its widespread application in irrigation systems, Neptune has become a staple for automating dam level control with remarkable efficiency. Typically, one or two devices are deployed for this purpose: one to monitor the dam level and the other to manage the activation of the water pump. These devices not only communicate with each other but also seamlessly connect with you and others through WhatsApp, providing real-time updates and control capabilities. Furthermore, they seamlessly integrate with our FineTrack control room, offering a comprehensive solution for overseeing dam operations. With Neptune's versatility and connectivity, you can ensure optimal water management with ease and confidence.

Water Flow Count Management

Experience precise water management like never before with our electronic water counter. Whether it's tracking irrigation usage or monitoring water levels in storage systems like Jojo tanks, our device provides accurate measurements and invaluable insights. Receive instant alerts when water usage approaches your preset thresholds or opt for daily updates on your consumption. With our FineTrack system, access detailed reports spanning daily, weekly, or monthly intervals to gain a comprehensive understanding of your water usage patterns. Paired with our level sensors, you'll effortlessly monitor fluctuations in your water tank or storage system, ensuring you're always prepared. Plus, with WhatsApp integration, control is at your fingertips, allowing you to manage everything from your smartphone with unparalleled ease and convenience. Say goodbye to water waste and hello to efficient, informed water management with our electronic water counter.

Poultry Farm Management

One of our esteemed customers operates a poultry farm with numerous chicken boilers, each housing thousands of chickens. Our cutting-edge systems play a crucial role in monitoring key parameters such as temperature, humidity, and the operation of fans, ensuring optimal conditions for poultry welfare. With a remarkable 250 devices installed on their farm, our latest innovation, the FineTrack control room, provides centralized monitoring capabilities, empowering farm managers to stay abreast of every aspect of farm operations. Additionally, we offer eSIM management services, streamlining connectivity management by handling airtime and data for all 250 devices under a single account. This tailored service is available to any customer utilizing a substantial number of our controllers, providing personalized solutions to meet unique needs efficiently. Whether you're overseeing a poultry farm or managing other agricultural operations, our integrated solutions guarantee seamless control and unparalleled peace of mind.

Pig Farm Management

Just as poultry farms rely on our Neptune controllers for efficient management, so do pig farms find them indispensable. With Neptune, pig farmers gain the ability to closely monitor critical factors such as temperature and humidity, while also enjoying the flexibility of customizable inputs to accommodate additional data needs. Seamlessly integrated with both WhatsApp and our FineTrack control room, Neptune offers unparalleled ease of control and monitoring. Experience the convenience of centralized management like never before, all while enjoying a sleek and modern interface. With Neptune, optimizing operations on pig farms has never been simpler or more visually appealing.
