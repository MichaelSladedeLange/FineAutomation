Wireless Passive Sensor

This wireless passive has a 12 meter detection range, 50 to 150 meter tranmitter range and 3-5 year battery life using battery saving features.

Features

  • Detection range: 12 meters and a 100 degree radius.
  • Transmitter range: 50 to 150 meters indoor.
  • Battery life: 3 - 5 years.
  • Powered by 6V lithium battery.
  • Battery saving feature.
  • 10Kg to 20Kg pet immunity.
  • DIP switch zone selection
  • Program to unit within seconds

Universal Wireless Transmitter/Door Guard

This wireless door guard has a transmitter range from 80-250 meters outdoors and battery life of 3 years. Has one normally closed input that will transmit to the Venus.
